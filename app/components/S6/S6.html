Developing an active lifestyle

    One way to develop an active lifestyle is to set aside a specific time for a formal exercise programme, however, there is a lot someone can do to achieve their desired levels of physical activity simply by building it into normal daily life. Here are some tips:

    • During work days, go for a walk in your lunch break. Aim to find at least three different walks and vary them throughout the week
    • Have business meetings 'on the hoof', i.e. conduct meetings with clients and colleagues whist walking around the building or local park, rather then sitting in an office
    • If you have children, exercise with your child. There are a vast number of options, e.g. swimming, playing in the garden or local park, cycling
    • When travelling to the shops by car, park several hundred metres away and walk the remaining distance, rather than finding a nearby parking space
    • Consider taking the stairs a floor or two instead of using the lift
